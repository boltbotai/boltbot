User Guidelines

Please follow these guidelines when interacting with BoltBot⚡ on Discord:

1. No Spamming or Flooding

Do not spam or flood BoltBot⚡ with excessive requests.

Reason: This disrupts the conversation and can negatively affect other users' experience. Use BoltBot⚡ responsibly.

2. No Offensive Content

Avoid posting or sharing offensive, hateful, or explicit content with BoltBot⚡.

Reason: Such content violates Discord's Terms of Service and harms the community. BoltBot⚡ aims to provide a positive and respectful experience.

3. No Impersonation or Harassment

Do not use BoltBot⚡ to impersonate or harass other users.

Reason: Impersonation and harassment are unacceptable and can lead to serious consequences. BoltBot⚡ is here to assist and enhance the server experience.

4. No Self-Promotion or Advertising

Refrain from using BoltBot⚡ for self-promotion, advertising, or soliciting.

Reason: Unsolicited promotions or requests are not permitted. BoltBot⚡ is intended to enrich conversations, not to serve as a promotional tool.

5. No Misuse of AI Capabilities

Do not attempt to manipulate or exploit BoltBot⚡ in ways that are inconsistent with its intended use.

Reason: Misusing or manipulating BoltBot⚡ undermines its purpose and can negatively affect its performance and user experience.

6. No Unauthorized Manipulation

Do not attempt to interfere with or alter BoltBot⚡'s internal workings.

Reason: Unauthorized manipulation can disrupt the bot's functionality and lead to severe consequences. BoltBot⚡ is designed to be a safe and enjoyable part of your server.
